Online/Social Media Litigation and Related Matters

  • The firm is currently representing a Facebook member publisher whose advertising revenues were wrongfully and without notice “de-monetized” by Facebook.
  • The firm is currently defending an anonymous online poster, whose identity is being sought by subpoena to the hosting website, for purposes of pursuing a defamation action against the poster.
  • The firm is currently representing the victim of persistent online harassment by a former spouse.
  • The firm recently succeeded in prevailing upon an online publisher to voluntarily   “de-list” a controverted news article for purposes of general online search.
  • The firm also recently prevailed on another online publisher to update a flawed and outdated article and to “re-index” the article to the update for search purposes.
