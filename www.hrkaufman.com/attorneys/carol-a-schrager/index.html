Carol A. Schrager

Of Counsel
Trial and Appellate Litigation

Carol Schrager maintains her own active practice in trial and appellate litigation and commercial and real estate transactions.  She has worked closely with Henry Kaufman, on various legal matters, dating back to 1982.  Ms. Schrager is currently the firm’s senior Of Counsel, in which capacity she regularly consults on a broad range of civil litigation matters in the firm’s media, publishing, first amendment and intellectual property law practice, advising on litigation strategy, discovery, motions, appellate practice and briefing, and related issues.

Current Practice (1985 – Present):
